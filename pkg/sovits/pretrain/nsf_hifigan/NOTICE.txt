--- DiffSinger Community Vocoder ---

ARCHITECTURE: NSF-HiFiGAN
RELEASE DATE: 2022-12-11

HYPER PARAMETERS:
 - 44100 sample rate
 - 128 mel bins
 - 512 hop size
 - 2048 window size
 - fmin at 40Hz
 - fmax at 16000Hz
